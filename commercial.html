Commercial Services

Elevators to save time

Office environments have unique needs around time. Rocket-Elevators is prepared to help manage planning for the morning, lunch, and afternoon rush with a myriad of tools around destination management and control. Rocket-Elevators is committed to help you design for your custom environment. Reducing the footprint of elevators in a commercial office building equals more free space for tenants and their work space needs. Smaller size does not mean sacrificing ride quality or door operation.
